Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a journalism background to apply? ▼

No. ACJ UNIBEN accepts students from all departments and all academic disciplines. We train our members from the ground up. What we look for is intellectual curiosity, a commitment to accuracy, and the willingness to be corrected.

Is there a membership fee? ▼

There is a nominal annual dues payment that covers production costs for The Campus Voice and member resources. The amount is confirmed at orientation and is kept at a level accessible to all students.

How much time will ACJ UNIBEN require? ▼

Expect to commit a minimum of four hours per week during the session. Investigative reporting and editorial board roles will require more. We do not recommend ACJ UNIBEN as a passive membership — it is an active commitment.

Can I join if I am in my final year? ▼

Yes, though we encourage you to join as early as possible to get the full benefit of training and publishing opportunities. Final-year students are eligible to apply and will be placed immediately upon acceptance.

Can I join if I am a postgraduate student? ▼

Yes. ACJ UNIBEN accepts postgraduate students and often places them in senior correspondent or editorial advisory roles given their research and analytical experience.
